WebLibre offers local, on-device AI inference to help you manage your containers more efficiently.
### Currently Available Features

- **Container Name Suggestions**  
  Get recommendations for naming your containers.
- **Tab Suggestions**  
  Receive suggestions for similar tabs based on your other container tabs.

### Things to Keep in Mind

- WebLibre uses a local AI model to analyze your open tab titles and suggest container tabs and names. All processing happens entirely on your device.
- AI enhancements operate entirely within your browser, keeping all data on your device. Local AI processing respects your privacy and provides faster suggestions for container groups and names. You can control this behavior anytime through settings.
- AI can sometimes make mistakes, so please review suggested group names and tab selections.
