**Bangs** are powerful shortcuts that allow you to search directly on other websites, bypassing the need for a third-party search engine. By using a bang, you are taken straight to the search results page of your chosen site.

## Built-in Bang Collection

WebLibre comes bundled with **thousands of bangs** for popular websites and services.The bang database is regularly updated to include new services and maintain accuracy.

## Custom Search Engines

You can create your own custom bangs for websites not included in the default collection.

## Bang Features

### Frequency Sorting

WebLibre tracks how often you use each bang and surfaces frequently-used bangs in autocomplete suggestions. The more you use a bang, the higher it appears in results.

## Privacy Benefits

Bangs enhance privacy by:

1. **Reducing Search Engine Queries** - Fewer queries means less data shared with search providers
2. **Direct Navigation** - Skip intermediary search result pages that track your clicks
3. **No Referrer Leakage** - Direct navigation reduces referrer header exposure
4. **Local Processing** - Bang matching happens on-device with no network overhead
