WebLibre comes with a local first approach: What can be found on device should not require contacting remote services.

The browser provides you with your own personal local search engine containing data from various sources:

- **[[Tab System]]**: Search through Website title, address and full content without size limit.
- **[[Web Feeds]]**: Aggregate your own personal choices of web feeds like news pages and blogs that are automatically kept up to date in background. Finding recent articles without the use of external search providers. The full content of articles is indexed as well.
- [[Bangs]]: Select bangs for direct on-site-search bypassing third party search engines
- Browsing History: Find previously visited websites by title and address.
