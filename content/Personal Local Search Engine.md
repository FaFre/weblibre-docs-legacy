**WebLibre** is designed with a **local-first philosophy**: whenever possible, your device handles search and retrieval, minimizing the need to contact remote services.

### Your Personal Local Search Engine

The browser acts as your own private search engine, indexing and searching data from multiple sources:

- **[[Tab Management]]**  
  Search across all open tabs by website title, address, and full page content—**with no size limits**.

- **[[Web Feeds]]**  
  Aggregate and search your personally selected web feeds, such as news sites and blogs. Feeds are automatically updated in the background, allowing you to **find recent articles without relying on external search providers**. The **full content of each article is indexed** for comprehensive search.

- **[[Bangs]]**  
  Use custom bangs for **direct on-site searches**, letting you bypass third-party search engines entirely.

- **Browsing History**  
  Instantly locate previously visited websites by searching their titles and addresses.

---

**Your data stays on your device—fast, private, and always available.**
